What is 3moji?

3moji is my attempt at creating a 3D Emoji library. During the beginning of the pandemic I started modeling Emoji. It was therapeutic and I enjoyed doing it. After making pretty much all of them I decided it would be good to pack them up and give them to others to use so I created 3moji.org. The website is a work in progress, but it is slowly getting better day by day.
